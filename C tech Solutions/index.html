Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have access to course materials?

Basic plan students get 6 months of access, Premium and Professional plan students get lifetime access to all course materials and updates.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer 3-month and 6-month installment plans with 0% interest. You can also apply for our scholarship program if you need financial assistance.

What's included in the job guarantee?

Premium and Professional plan graduates who complete all course requirements and participate in our career services program are guaranteed to receive a job offer within 6 months or receive a full refund.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time by paying the difference. Downgrades are available within the first 30 days of enrollment.
